- About -

Hi!
My name is Audrey Bembenek

For as long as I remember I have enjoyed the elements of design, whether it was fashioning the perfect dream house for Barbie, a modern Lego Friends city (with its cute buildings and outdoor spaces) or designing a Webkinz "World" for my plush pets. As I got older, my creativity was directed toward playing the piano, dabbling in photography and designing with a crochet hook or Cricut. Since high school graduation, I have been employed as an e-commerce personal shopper, as well as working towards a degree in mathematics. While exploring ways to bridge the world of technology with an interest in design, mathematics, and working with others, the opportunity to learn web development presented itself. Web development has been a way to combine the enjoyment of problem solving with an outlet for my creativity. It would be a privilege to help others reach their business goals through an effective online presence. I am passionate about the promotion of truly authentic brands. Please feel free to reach out to me with your web development questions or if you would simply like a fresh set of eyes to help communicate your company’s message.
